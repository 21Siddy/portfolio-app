The domain oldisserving.live is managed by Cloudflare. Instead of opening risky ports on my home router, a Cloudflare Tunnel (Lenovo BareMetal Server) creates a secure, encrypted outbound pipe from my apartment to the global edge.

Coolify acts as an open-source Heroku. It listens for a Webhook from GitHub, pulls the latest code, and builds a fresh Nixpacks container without me ever touching a deploy button.
